About Fly Easy

Founded in the heart of Zimbabwe, Fly Easy is dedicated to making global travel accessible and affordable for everyone. We understand the complexities of finding the best travel experiences, and our mission is to simplify this process for you by crafting personalized itineraries.

Our team of expert travel consultants will work closely with you to understand your preferences, budget, and dreams, then meticulously plan every detail of your journey, from unique accommodations to unforgettable activities.

Whether you're planning a business trip, a family vacation, or an adventurous solo journey, Fly Easy is your reliable partner for discovering and experiencing the best of any corner of the globe.

Our Travel Services

Our Agency's main objective is to provide professional travel services acting as an effective link between the customers we serve and the principal travel suppliers such as airlines, hotels, road transport operators, tour and cruise providers. For this purpose, we have employed a team of highly qualified and experienced Travel Consultants with the necessary knowledge to handle any aspect of travel.

Air Reservations

  • Plan itineraries with at least three options (domestic/international).
  • Provide quotes, discuss hidden stops, layovers, restrictions, and special conditions for changes, cancellation, and refunds.
  • Share baggage allowance and excess baggage charges.
  • Issue and deliver tickets, provide advanced seating plans.

Hotel Accommodation

We reserve, pay, and issue confirmation vouchers to our customers for various hotels both domestic and international.

Tours, Excursions, Inclusive Tours

We plan and arrange tours and excursions both inbound and outbound and sell Inclusive and Packaged tours.

Car Hire Services

We facilitate, book, and confirm car rental services anywhere in the world.

Airport Services

  • Provide meet and assist services, including VIP Services at the airport.
  • Facilitate Airport transfer services from designated points to and from airports to hotels anywhere in the world.
  • Provide information on conditions for access to airport lounges for non-qualifying tickets, accept payments, and provide vouchers.

Visa Processing

We provide assistance with visa process information in line with existing immigration rules. Where immigration rules require clients' personal appearance, we provide all the necessary information to enable a speedy processing of the visa by the relevant authorities.

Travel Insurance

We provide travel insurance to cover any of the following travel risks:

  • Ticket Loss.
  • Travel Interruptions.
  • Flight Delays and Cancellations.
  • Baggage Irregularities and Baggage Loss.
  • Medical Care resulting from accidents or general sickness.

Travel Information

  • Provide destination information including weather conditions and information on security issues.
  • Provide periodic information on special fare offers by various suppliers including airlines and hotels.
